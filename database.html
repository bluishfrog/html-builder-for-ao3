Configuration of Your Database

This is where you set up and manage the files that store your content and account info. Everything stays on your computer; nothing is automatically uploaded or saved online. Make sure to download your files regularly while you work, so you don’t lose any progress. Think of this page as your little control center for keeping things organized.
